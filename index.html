About me

I've always been passionate about Life science and informatics, and pursued a PhD in Cell biology from the University of Versailles Saint Quentin-en-Yvelines (UVSQ) in France.
During these four years, I tried to understand and characterize cell signaling pathways involved in cancer in a cellular in vitro model, and also teached cell biology and genetics to B.Sc. and Master's degree students at UVSQ.

After caring for my late grandmother, I furthered my scientific career by taking a postdoctoral position at the Gustave Roussy Institute in Villejuif. I wanted to initiate a shift towards informatics, which had always been an interest of mine and this research project was the opportunity to strengthen my knowledge in bioinformatics and data analysis.

This experience comforted my desire to use my scientific knowledge and skills to fully shift towards informatics and particularly data science and deep learning. I thus completed a comprehensive Data Scientist training (Master's equivalency from Paris I Panthéon-Sorbonne University). I'm now looking for a Data Scientist position in the healthcare field where both aspects of my training will make me an asset to your team!
